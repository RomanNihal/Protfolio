About Me

I am a Computer Science and Engineering graduate from Green University of Bangladesh, currently working as a Research Assistant in the Department of Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at the same institute.

My research focuses on QR code optimization, handwritten character recognition for low-resource languages, and efficient object detection in remote sensing. Beyond academic research, I specialize in building practical AI solutions, including context-aware systems using RAG frameworks and autonomous AI Agents powered by the Google Agent Development Kit (ADK).
